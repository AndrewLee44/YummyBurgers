About Us

Yummy Burgers is a fast-growing fast-food chain, serving customers since the 2000s. It started off as a single fast-food store in Ang Mo Kio, Singapore. From 2005 to 2015, they slowly expanded their business and now there is an outlet in almost every estate in Singapore. It is gradually becoming the biggest fast-food restaurant in Singapore. Products offered by Yummy Burgers are fast food items such as burgers and beverages.
